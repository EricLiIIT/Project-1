My dog, Lottie

My dog is a pitbull-labrador mixed dog. We adopted her 5 years ago when she was a puppy from the Anti-Cruelty Society in Chicago.

She is an energetic dog and loves to play fetch. She's difficult to be introduced to but she is very affectionate once she is.

She's a very curious dog, and loves to meet new animals.
